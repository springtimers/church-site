Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

There is no dress code at Christ Embassy Moscow for members or guests. A specific attire is not required, and our members wear a variety of styles of clothing. We think it makes sense to wear nice, clean, modest clothing to church. As long as you do that you will not feel out of place.

Do you have a nursery?

Yes. Nursery is provided during all services for children 2 years of age and under.

What are your services like?

Our services are what is commonly called “contemporary.” The worship music, led by our Praise Team, is electrifying and spiritually uplifting. We do have special music from members of the congregation, children and special guest artists on occasion. The focus of all of our meetings is the Word of God. Generally, we will have prayer, worship, devotional reading from Rhapsody of Realities daily devotional, video announcements with cutting edge graphics, an offering, and then the teaching from the Bible.

What should I wear?

There is no dress code at Christ Embassy Moscow for members or guests. A specific attire is not required, and our members wear a variety of styles of clothing. We think it makes sense to wear nice, clean, modest clothing to church. As long as you do that you will not feel out of place.

Do visitors have to stand up and say anything?

No, you sure don’t. We will give you a connection card and ask you to give it to one of our guest services host or hostess at our meet and greet after service. We do this so we can have a record of your visit. We do have a free gift for each guest at our meet and greet.

Do you have other questions ?

If there’s something we missed on this list, just give us a call on +7 992 444 07 70 or ask someone when you arrive. Whether you’re new to the area and looking for a church home, or if it’s been a long time since you’ve attended church at all, we hope your first visit at Christ Embassy will be awesome! We exist to serve you and your family, and we are looking forward to meeting you personally. We hope you’ll feel right at home from the very first moment! Let us know how we can serve you better, and we hope to meet you this Sunday!
