LMAM

The Loveworld Music and Art Ministry is an important part/arm of the church.We minister God's word in songs and lead the congregation in a time of praise and worship to God. Music (Praise and Worship) is a type of prayer in fact it is the highest form of prayer and one of the most effective forms of spiritual warfare.

‍Psalm 150:6 says “Let everything that has breath praise the Lord”.
‍God inhabits/lives in the praises of his people (Psalms 22:3).

Music creates the divine atmosphere for God to come and manifest Himself in glory, power and love. 

You do not have to have the best voice in the world to join the music department. We will train you.

TECHNICAL TEAM

The media team comprises the audio visual and data arm of the ministry supporting the pastorate in the delivery of the message using modern multimedia technology.

Our vision is to effectively deliver multimedia networks as a support service to the ministry vision in taking the divine presence of God to the world.

Our mission is to implement the correct and appropriate technologies to fulfill the vision. This is done by continually developing the level of skill of the members in every aspect of the media team.

USHERS

“And God has appointed these in the church: first apostles, second prophets, third teachers, after that miracles, then gifts of healings, helps, administrations, varieties of tongues.” 1 Cor 12:28 (New King James Version)

‍The usher is the first contact for the congregation as well as visitors in church, and in many cases, the first impression experienced is that of the ushers.

It is therefore our goal that you are welcomed and the environment in church is conducive for you to hear the word of God.

The ushering department falls within the category of the Ministry of Helps. The department is responsible for maintaining an atmosphere of reverence and order before, during and after the service.

LOVEWORLD PUBLICATIONS

This department is focused on reaching out  with the gospel, building up saints with excellence and clarity.

Faith comes as you hear the word of God hence as a department, we are committed to making available our ministry books, video tapes, audio tapes and CDs for the spiritual growth and edification of the saints and for the salvation of men.

In order to take our unique messages to all the peoples and nations of the earth, the LoveWorld publications department of Christ Embassy Russia has the following units:
1. The Bookshop – an outlet where our materials are displayed.
2. Free distribution Unit – This unit is committed to distributing our best seller, Rhapsody of Realities to different outlets, thus carrying God’s divine presence to these places.

PRAYER TEAM

“And he spake a parable unto them to this end, that men ought always to pray , and not to faint.” Luke 18:1 ( King James Version)
‍
The prayer team is a group of brethren who are constantly interceding for the saints, they receive and pray for concerns and requests from individuals in the church.

They also work with the Pastor  to provide opportunities for the congregation to enrich their prayer lives.
